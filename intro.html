The South is now understood as a place to be dreamt about but also as a space that is struggling because of its past and its present.

There’s a force pulling students from the Global South to come to the North as a prescribed path to success.

 

I’ve set out to listen to these unique voices. Their ideas of what they thought they would find in the north, against what they’ve found; What they left behind, and what they’re longing for. They might think they have found something, or they could have got lost in their journey.

 

The collection of stories expresses the complexity and diversity behind their motivations, their goals and their expectations.
