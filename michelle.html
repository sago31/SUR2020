Woke up outperformed, geographically

disconnected: her body contracts and looses freedoms. She flies with caution. So clumsy and impulsive at the same time, keeps falling into the same trap. Only knowledge, both of the territory and the soul, can make the movement of her body blossom. They ask, where are you from? — But she can’t perform her identities in ways that are satisfying to others.

 

And again, new spaces validate new dynamics and walk you through semi-awake delirium. A new window, a new door, you watch the pleasant movement of the clouds, the heavy scattering of the rain. You hold your phone in your hand and take pictures of interiors, never of exteriors. You feel this city belongs to visitors. Then you detach, living in that constant state of delirium, it’s hard to understand that temperature matters so much. That the snow is burning your skin and that you must, by any means, feel the light wind that comes with a warm place by the sea.

 

Ferociously devoted to daydream while

notifications tic—toc this day.

 

As you arrive to that new land, you repeat the ritual, you perform the stories that are the platform of your identity. Get into the swamp, walk in the grass, you still feel the deep roots of girlhood, you are wild, stubborn and impulsive, if you are made of something, it’s of desire. Tame the fire — you don’t own that land, you can’t walk behind those fences.

 

Escaped from the mountains to swim to the other side of the lake. After all that effort, you see that you are just an eternal mirror of yourself.
